My Books

First book is Dune. It follows young Paul Atreides, whose family takes control of Arrakis, a harsh desert planet and the universe's only source of "melange" or "spice"—a vital drug for space travel and extending life. My 2nd, has to be Project Hail Mary. A brilliant, fast-paced sci-fi adventure about an astronaut waking up with amnesia and the fate of humanity resting on his shoulders. My third book is The Odyssey by Homer. Homer’s great epic of a hero’s journey home. Hang tight to see a few other surprise books!
